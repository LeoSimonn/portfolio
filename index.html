About Me

Where design meets
technology & ambition

I'm an entrepreneur at heart, driven by the intersection of design, AI, and automation. My mission: transform businesses into memorable experiences by building products with purpose, usability, and performance.

My journey began in childhood in a family immersed in design — from CorelDraw and Photoshop to SketchUp and Enscape. Today that foundation flows into Figma for UI/UX and low-code platforms like n8n, Make, and Lovable to ship products fast.

Current Role

UI/UX Designer Intern

Apple Developer Academy · PUCRS

Across Academy projects I lead UX flows, interfaces, visual identity, and product decisions — and step into code when design needs to meet the shipped app. InsightUp was my solo build; every other app was a 5-person, cross-functional team effort.

I also bring AI into solutions and into the room: helping teammates use it strategically. On Clairfy and RetroTrip I served as Product Owner — priorities, vision, and alignment between experience and development.

Real Impact

120+ Store Designs

Novaletra · Tintas Renner by PPG

Through Novaletra Comunicação Visual, I create 3D architectural visualizations for Tintas Renner — an iconic Brazilian paints brand acquired by PPG Industries (US global leader in coatings, based in Gravataí/RS).

While 3D isn't my primary passion, the results speak for themselves: 120+ stores launched and 35% production increase.
